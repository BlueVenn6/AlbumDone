The app attempts to use the system trash or an app-managed fallback trash directory where possible, but recovery is not guaranteed in every environment.

## Custom Endpoint Risk

If you enter a third-party proxy or custom endpoint, make sure the service is trustworthy before sending screenshots, private photos, customer data, or sensitive instructions through it.
